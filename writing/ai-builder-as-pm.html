The PM in the Age of AI Builders: Where Collaboration Ends AI builder 时代的 PM：协同的边界

July 2026  ·  a short essay 2026 年 7 月  ·  短文

After months of building products in pair with AI, my biggest takeaway isn't “it's faster.” It's that you have to know where the boundary of your own thinking sits. AI can carry the implementation, but what drives the collaboration has to be your own structured thinking and methodology — what to build, to what standard, and how it will be accepted. Hand those judgments over and the collaboration falls apart. Decisions and product judgment are the part you cannot outsource.

The second shift is relational. Product and engineering are no longer upstream and downstream — we co-develop. The pipeline is shorter, iteration is faster, and things that used to wait for a sprint now show running results the same day. But light-speed iteration has a trap: it must never come at the cost of product thinking. The faster the loop, the better the division of labor with AI has to be — I've even started asking the AI to proactively remind me what, standing in the product seat, I should be doing right now. A tool that guards your thinking is a shape of collaboration that didn't exist before.

The last one is altitude. Pairing with AI for long stretches, it's easy to sink into the concrete business scenario and the implementation details. What actually works is stepping out and looking at the complete pipeline — from the abstract to the concrete, and back to the abstract. First get clear on what the whole chain needs to be true, then drop into one link and build it, then lift your head and check where it sits in the chain.

AI has made “getting it built” unprecedentedly cheap. Which is exactly why “getting it thought through” has never been worth more.

与 AI 结对做产品这几个月，我最大的体会不是 "效率高了"，而是必须先意识到思考的边界在哪里。 AI 可以承担实现，但带动协同的必须是我自己的结构化思考和方法论 —— 什么该做、做到什么程度、验收标准是什么。这些判断一旦交出去，协同就散了。 决策与产品把关，是不能外包的那一部分。

第二个变化发生在关系上。产品和研发不再是上下游，而是共同开发：链路缩短，迭代提速， 很多过去要排期的事情，现在当天就能看到运行结果。但光速迭代有一个陷阱 —— 它不能以牺牲产品思考为代价。循环越快，越需要与 AI 更好地分工； 我甚至开始让 AI 主动提醒我：站在产品立场，此刻该做的事是什么。 工具反过来守护思考，这是以前没有过的协作形态。

最后一条是视角。与 AI 结对久了，很容易陷进具体的业务场景和实现细节里。 真正有效的办法是跳脱出来，从完整链路看问题 —— 从抽象到具体，再回到抽象。 先想清楚整条链路要成立什么，再落到某一个环节去做，做完再抬头，检查它在链上的位置。

AI 让 "做出来" 变得空前便宜。正因为如此，"想清楚" 变得空前值钱。
