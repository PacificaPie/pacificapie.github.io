The problem

Campus-recruiting information is scattered across sources, and the work of tracking it is mostly repeated labor. The requirement I set for myself: the entire daily loop — see a new opening → judge it → produce a résumé → apply — has to finish in under 10 minutes a day.

My call

For a personal tool, the core metric isn't feature count — it's the friction of the daily loop. And two human-in-the-loop rules are non-negotiable: applications are never auto-submitted, and every number on a résumé must trace back to a ledger of verified facts.

What shipped

  • Multi-source job discovery — structured tables + recruiting-platform APIs.
  • Profile-based filtering and dedup ahead of anything expensive.
  • LLM-agent enrichment — finds the official application entry, pulls the JD, and judges the match.
  • Daily scheduled digest pushed on a fixed cadence.
  • Full-pipeline failure alerting, with alerting-channel credentials kept separate from data-channel credentials.
  • Cross-channel suppression of jobs already applied to.

Validation

The pipeline has been running unattended since June 2026, on two scheduled runs per day. The alerting mechanism has already caught — and led to fixes for — three distinct classes of environment failure.

What iterating taught me

In an automated system, “no news” and “no hits” must never be indistinguishable. That ambiguity is the cardinal sin of automation.
The biggest enemy of accumulation isn't the tool — it's friction.

问题

校招信息散落在各处，跟进它们的工作大部分是重复劳动。我给自己定的要求是： 每天 10 分钟内完成 "看到新岗 → 判断 → 出简历 → 投出" 这整个循环。

我的判断

个人工具的核心指标不是功能数量，是"每日循环的摩擦"。 另外有两条 human-in-the-loop 铁律不可协商：投递不自动提交； 简历上每个数字必须可溯源到事实台账。

交付

  • 多信源岗位发现 —— 结构化表 + 招聘平台 API。
  • 画像筛选去重，放在一切昂贵操作之前。
  • LLM agent 富化 —— 搜官方入口、抓 JD、判匹配度。
  • 每日定时推送。
  • 全链路故障报警，报警通道与数据通道凭证分离。
  • 已投岗位跨渠道自动抑制。

验证

系统自 2026 年 6 月起无人值守运行中，每日两班。 曾靠报警机制发现并修复三类环境故障。

认知迭代

"没消息" 和 "没命中" 不可区分，是自动化系统的大忌。
沉淀的最大敌人是摩擦，不是工具。
