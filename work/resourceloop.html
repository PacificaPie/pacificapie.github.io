ResourceLoop — a B-Side System Delivered Pairing with AI ResourceLoop —— 与 AI 结对交付的 B 端系统

Internship · Meituan Internal Applications
internship project (internal)  ·  product shape, interaction, and mechanism proposed by me, validated by the team, co-developed  ·  Internal 实习项目（内部）  ·  产品形态 / 交互 / 机制由我先提出、团队校验后协同开发  ·  内部

The problem

Resource requests and reviews for trending events had no systematic process — the request-and-approval loop lived nowhere in particular.

My call

The product shape, interaction, and mechanism came from me first; the team validated them and we built together. Implementation was paired with AI — with me owning direction, judgment, and acceptance.

What shipped

  • A three-role system: request → review → deploy.
  • Incentive-mechanism design — success rates semi-visible to prevent gaming; appeals decoupled from review, with scorecards for two-way accountability.
  • Multiple iterations driven by user feedback — an event calendar with a pinned request zone, explicit draft-state backfill, and error messages rewritten in plain human language.

Validation

A launchable system, iterated over multiple rounds. A frontend engineering baseline was part of the delivery — unit tests caught a real Number(null) === 0 bug on the spot.

What iterating taught me

Working with AI has to be driven by your own structured thinking. A PM can take part in development — but never at the cost of time spent thinking about the big direction.

问题

热点事件的资源申请—审核没有系统化流程，申请与审批的循环无处安放。

我的判断

产品形态、交互、机制由我先提出，团队校验后协同开发。 实现借助 AI 结对 —— 我定方向、判断与验收。

交付

  • 三角色系统：申报 → 审核 → 投放。
  • 激励机制设计 —— 成功率半外显防套利；申诉与审核解耦 + 记分卡双向问责。
  • 按用户反馈多轮迭代 —— 事件日历 + 钉死申报区、 草稿态显式回填、报错用人话外显。

验证

可上线系统，多轮迭代。交付里包含前端工程化基线 —— 单测当场抓到 Number(null) === 0 的真 bug。

认知迭代

与 AI 协同要用结构化思考带动；产品可以参与开发，但不能牺牲对大方向的思考时间。
