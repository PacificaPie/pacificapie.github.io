Cross-border Commerce, Zero to One — an AI-driven sourcing-to-listing system 跨境电商从 0 到 1 —— AI 驱动的选品-上架全流程体系

Personal Project Building Applications
An independent TikTok Shop (Malaysia) business, run on a six-stage AI pipeline: research → selection → sourcing → pricing → creative → listing. 独立运营的马来西亚 TikTok Shop 店铺，跑在六环节 AI 流水线上：调研→选品→货源→定价→素材→上架。

The problem

Cross-border selling looks like an information game but is actually an execution game. I wanted to test whether one person plus AI could run the entire chain — with real money, a real store, and real inventory decisions.

My call

Division of labor: I own the irreversible decisions (market / platform / category choice, budget allocation, risk judgment) and the platform-side operations (business license, store setup, ERP/API activation); AI takes on data collection and tool-chain coding. Let data veto intuition — the hair-accessory category was dropped once the data confirmed its market structure and loss-leader monopoly; the real demand shape of “sport phone cases” in Malaysia turned out to be waterproof pouches. Identified and avoided categories monopolized by RM0.01 loss-leaders, and chose an open niche: high ticket, zero loss-leaders. Risk screening was pushed forward into the search terms themselves: patent minefields, high-return categories, and shipping-restricted goods are excluded at the data source — not caught by manual review.

What shipped

Delivered in 12 days: a product-selection shortlist across six product lines (21 qualified suppliers, est. profit ¥8–26 per unit); the four-layer selection funnel packaged as a reusable skill with experience write-back; the ERP open-platform API path tested end to end (HmacSHA256 signing aligned byte-for-byte with the official algorithm; 2 products in the TikTok publish queue); a two-layer product-image factory (AI image-to-image scene backgrounds + a deterministic label-rendering layer); and a bilingual Malay listing copy system. About 950 lines of Python.

Validation

Three-source cross-validation: Google Trends momentum × TikTok Shop market data (via a third-party data service) × 1688 wholesale data (dropship price / lead time / repeat-purchase rate). Data-quality engineering: sparse trend sub-terms distorted the signal, so I switched to a 12-month weekly window with sparsity flags; one-cent loss-leaders polluted price-band statistics, so a floor filter marks them separately; reseller listings with “high margin, zero sales” were fingerprinted by their identical repeat-rate/MOQ patterns, and “sample purchase before commitment” became a hard rule. Cost engineering: comparable data services differ in price by 33× — compared and chose accordingly, keeping monthly data cost within a $5 free tier. The store is open and bound to the ERP; currently in the sample-and-inspect stage.

What iterating taught me

From “finding one winning product” to “building reusable product-selection infrastructure” — in the AI era, a solo operator's core leverage is a repeatable pipeline. Data vetoes intuition, and data gets corrected too: only when both directions have happened can you call it data-driven.

问题

跨境电商看起来是信息差生意,实际是执行节奏生意。我想验证:一个人加 AI,能不能跑通完整链路——真金白银、真店铺、真库存决策。

我的判断

分工边界:我负责不可逆决策(市场/平台/品类选择、预算分配、风险判断)与平台侧运营(执照、店铺、ERP/API 开通),AI 承担数据抓取与工具链编码。用数据否决直觉——发饰品类因市场特性与引流款垄断被数据证实放弃;"运动手机壳"在马来的真实需求形态是防水袋。识别 RM0.01 引流款垄断的品类并规避,选择高客单、零引流款的空位品类。风控前置到搜索词:专利雷区、高退货品类、物流受限品在数据源头排除,而不是靠人工复核。

交付

12 天交付:六条产品线选品清单(21 个达标货源,单件预估利润 ¥8-26)、四层选品漏斗封装为带经验回写的可复用 skill、ERP 开放平台 API 全链路实测跑通(HmacSHA256 签名与官方算法逐字节对齐,2 个商品进入 TikTok 待发布队列)、两层商品图工厂(AI 图生图场景底图 + 确定性标签层渲染)、马来语双语 listing 文案体系。约 950 行 Python。

验证

三源数据交叉验证:Google Trends 趋势动量 × TikTok Shop 市场数据(经第三方数据服务) × 1688 批发数据(代发价/时效/回头率)。数据质量工程:趋势细分词稀疏失真改 12 个月周度窗口+稀疏标记;一分钱引流款污染价格带统计,设下限过滤单独标注;分销盘"高利润零销量"链接用回头率/MOQ 雷同特征指纹识别,立"买样验证为硬前提"规则。成本工程:同类数据服务价差 33 倍对比择优,月度数据成本控制在 $5 免费额度内。店铺已开设并绑定 ERP,当前处于买样验货阶段。

认知迭代

从"找一个爆品"到"搭一套可复用的选品基础设施"——AI 时代个体户的核心杠杆是可重复的流水线。数据否决直觉,也修正数据:两个方向都发生过,才叫数据驱动。
